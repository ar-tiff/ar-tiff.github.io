Among the Unhurried

2026 | Illustration | Mixed Media

Under the fast-paced lifestyle, I explore scenes only visible when we slow down. My work documents an environment that allows us to exist without the pressure of efficiency. Through these unhurried moments of people, animals, and nature, I invite the audience to find a moment of peace and the space to be themselves simply.
