The Book of Rococo Closet

2025 | A4 Zine | Digital Illustration

Deconstructing the Rococo Silhouette is an illustrated A4 zine that peels back the layers of 18th-century French court dresses. Through interactive flaps, readers explore a virtual closet—discovering the purpose of each garment piece, how it shaped the iconic silhouette, and the history behind the era.
