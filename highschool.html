Fish Lantern

2022 | Installation | mixed media

The Fish Lantern explores the traditional culture of celebrating the Lantern Festival. Inspired by the coastal history of my hometown, this project looks at how people living near the sea create fish lanterns to pray for safety and a good harvest. The lantern installation, followed by watercolor pieces, spreads the understanding of this unique culture.
